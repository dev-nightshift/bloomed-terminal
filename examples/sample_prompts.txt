# Bloomed Terminal — sample prompts

Write a tight, two-sentence vignette about neon rain beading on old glass; keep it concrete and grounded.

Describe a corridor light stuttering back to life in one sentence; avoid melodrama.

Give me a short terminal-core note about a humming vent at night; include one specific sensory detail.

Compose two sentences about an empty security desk and a clock that runs a minute fast; stay understated.

One sentence: the elevator opens to a floor that shouldn’t exist; keep it subtle, no horror clichés.
